###证明$(e^x)^{‘} = e^x$
$\Delta y = e^{ x + \Delta x}-e^x = e^x(e^{\Delta x} - 1)$
$\lim\limits_ {\Delta x \to 0} \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = \lim\limits_ {\Delta x \to 0} e^x \frac{e^{\Delta x}-1}{\Delta x}$
令$t=e^{\Delta x}-1$，
则$\lim\limits_ {\Delta x \to 0} \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}=\lim\limits_ {t \to 0}{e^x \frac{t}{\ln{(t+1)}}}$
$=e^x \lim\limits_ {t \to 0 }\frac{1}{\ln{(t+1)^{\frac{1}{t}}}} = e^x \frac{1}{\ln e} = e^x$

###用微分的方法得到一些函数的近似值
假设$y=f(x)$在$x=x_0$处可微，则当$\Delta x \to 0$ 时，可以得到
$$f(x_0+\Delta x) = f(x_0)+f^’ (x_0)\Delta x+o(\Delta x)$$
$o(\Delta x)$是比$\Delta x$更高阶的无穷小量，这里可以把它忽略不计
应用这个操作，即$f(x_0+\Delta x) \approx f(x_0)+f^’ (x_0)\Delta x$,就可以得到
$$e^x \approx e^(0+x)=e^0+e^0 x=1+x$$
$$\ln (1+x) \approx \ln 1 +\frac{1}{1}x=x$$
$$(1+x)^{\alpha} \approx 1^{\alpha}+\alpha · 1^{\alpha-1} x =1+\alpha x$$
